Págame API v2.0.0

What is Págame?

About

Págame is a young SaaS company determined to facilitate digital transactions between people, businesses, and banks. Págame was founded with the core mission of increasing financial inclusivity in Bolivia, and all functionality is designed and developed in alignment with this mission.

This API was developed by the Pagame engineering team. Please contact brendan@pagame.org with any inquiries.

History

Págame was founded in the Summer of 2020 by three Wharton students with connections to Bolivia and the greater Latin American region, motivated by the following statistic: In Bolivia, over 70% of the population has access to smartphones, yet less than 30% own and actively operate a formal bank account.

Currently, P2P transactions require an insurmountable amount of information, to be manually entered by the user. This tedious process simply isn't necessary (it can be streamlined with an efficient and secure data retrieval system), and it's responsible for a nation-wide overdependence on cash. This overdependence is responsible for the following risks:

  • Transmission of contaminated paper currency have been shown to significantly contribute to the spread of viral diseases (including COVID-19)
  • Household savings in the form of cash are exposed to risk of robbery/burglary
  • Citizens without formal financial history cannot be evaluated for loans/credit offerings
  • Large informal sector of the Bolivian economy limits precision of GDP measurement and further hinders large-scale economic growth
  • The country faces increasing inability to grow transaction volume due to existing P2P payment barriers

Solution

Págame aims to mitigate the above risks by offering a suite of software solutions for a digital and inclusive financial network. The core value of Pagame is the ability to efficiently and securely organize, filter, and query data required for financial transactions. Págame innovates transaction processes by decreasing the amount of input required from a user. Upon sign up, a user will receive a one-time prompt to input the necessary information to operate their funds. After which, Pagame will retrieve all (non-specific to action) data to provide a supreme user experience in the transaction process.

What is included in this documentation?

This document describes the functions and features provided by the Pagame client API v2.0.0. Pagame builds upon this API with powerful mobile interfaces to deliver an unmatched user experience. However, we also acknowledge that a lack of collaborative innovation is, in part, responsible for the lack of digital coherence in the financial system. Thus, the Pagame API can work to bridge software communication between distinct financial networks. We sincerely welcome developers, engineers, and members of Bolivian financial institutions to explore our client API. Please reach out if you are interested in developing financial software on top of our foundational data network.
